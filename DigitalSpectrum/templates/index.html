Automating agriculture


Digital Spectrum Technologies (DST) is set up for any hydroponic, aeroponic, or soil based system. By gathering a variety of metrics about the environment, the system calibrates, modifies, and sustains the most optimal conditions to produce the highest yield for a particular crop.


Sensors include pH recordings, barometric data, humidity, moisture, and more! Effectively monitoring within an indoor system will log all resources that are accounted for and the plant receives exactly what it needs in order to thrive. Nothing is wasted. Everything is recycled.
